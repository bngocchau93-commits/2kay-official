  Stepping through the locked doors of the past, the grand lobby of Mrs. Ann's House welcomes lost souls with a hauntingly lavish embrace. 

  This space breathes a heavy blend of classic Art Deco and dark Gothic aesthetics—aristocratic and magnificent, yet brimming with eerie, cinematic shadows. The faint, golden glow of the chandelier spills over the deep teal walls, struggling to push back the darkness that constantly threatens to swallow the room. Meanwhile, the cold, rhythmic ticking of the antique grandfather clock echoes like a grim whisper: time in Mina Drumarsstad has either ground to a halt, or you are simply counting down the seconds to your own demise.

  A breathtakingly charming yet terrifying elegance, where luxury and invisible dread melt into one. Dare you take the next step up the stairs to uncover what lies ahead?
